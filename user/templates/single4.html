New Regulatory Data Reveals Extensive Price Discrimination in the FX Derivatives Market

By Colombus Expert  -  February 01, 2024

The FX derivatives market has long been characterized by a lack of transparency, particularly in the realm of discriminatory pricing. However, new regulatory data has brought to light a disconcerting reality: non-financial clients are subject to significant price discrimination, resulting in substantial transaction cost differentials. This discriminatory pricing has profound implications for both the financial sector and the broader economy.

RFQ Platforms: A Solution to Discriminatory Pricing in the FX Derivatives Market

The FX derivatives market has long been characterized by discriminatory pricing, with less sophisticated clients bearing the brunt of substantially higher spreads. However, the use of multi-dealer request-for-quote (RFQ) platforms has been found to eliminate price discrimination based on client sophistication.
